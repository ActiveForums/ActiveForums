<p class="Owner"><b>About DotNetNuke Active Forums</b></p>
Active Forums is an award winning, DotNetNuke module that allows you to easily build a complete on-line community. By leveraging the DotNetNuke framework, Active Forums allows you to quickly setup a feature-rich discussion forum for your website. Whether you are a hobbyist who just needs a single forum or building an on-line community for your enterprise, Active Forums is your solution.
